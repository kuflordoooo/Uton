# utoñ

**utoñ**  is a fast, single-file
arcade bullet-hell built in HTML5 Canvas — a neon-dusk **masquerade shooter** with
African roots and a play-to-earn layer on **Solana**.

You face a masquerade spirit across the cities, deserts and seas of the world. Dodge,
survive, defeat it, and chase a high score worth real **$utoñ** rewards.

**▶ Play now:** https://kuflordoooo.github.io/Uton/

---

## About the game

You pilot a lone hero at the bottom of the screen who **fires automatically**. Above you
hovers a masquerade boss that hovers, dives, and unleashes bullet patterns. Your only job
is to **move and survive** — drag your finger (or mouse) to weave through the danger. Bring
the boss down and you advance to the next world stage; the masquerade returns, tougher,
somewhere new.

It's designed to be **instantly playable on any phone**, with no install, no tutorial, required to start.

---

## How to play

- **Drag to move** — your blaster fires on its own, so focus entirely on dodging.
- **Defeat the masquerade** to clear a stage and travel to the next city.
- **Collect drops** for power-ups, gems and credits.
- **Tap your control buttons** (bomb, shield, extra life) when you're in trouble.
- **Pause anytime** to breathe, or to visit the upgrade store.

## Game features

### 29 world stages
Every stage is a hand-drawn dusk skyline with its own colour palette and signature landmark,
cycling through real places and wild nature:

> Lagos · London · Paris · Tokyo · Dubai · Doha · New York · Dublin · Buenos Aires ·
> Rio de Janeiro · São Paulo · Lima · Bogotá · Santiago · Shanghai · Singapore · Mumbai ·
> Bangkok · Seoul · Hong Kong · Sydney · Los Angeles · Chicago · Toronto · Mexico City ·
> San Francisco · the Desert · the River · the Open Sea

### Boss fights & surprises
- A masquerade boss that **hovers, telegraphs, and dives** to swallow you — dodge or shoot it down.
- **Mini-bosses every 4th level** with extra health and a ring-burst entrance.
- **Surprise attack patterns** that break the rhythm: ring bursts, bullet walls with a single gap, spirit comets, and crossfire.

### Power-up drops
Knock the boss down (and grab mid-fight drops) for timed boosts and surprises:
- **Rapid fire** — shoot faster
- **Spread shot** — extra bolts in a fan
- **Magnet** — pull in gems and credits
- **Burn** — set the masquerade on fire for damage over time
- **Invulnerable** — 5 seconds completely untouchable
- **Costumes** *(rare)* — your hero throws on a random fun look: top hat, crown, party hat, shades, halo, cowboy hat, or a propeller beanie

### Consumables
Earned as you climb, used when you need them:
- **Bomb** — clear the screen and hammer the boss
- **Shield** — a few seconds of protection
- **Extra life** — banked and spent on your terms

### Pickups
- **Gems** — boost your score
- **Credits** — the currency you spend in the store

## The Store

Credits you collect during a run are spent in the **upgrade store**, which opens
**automatically every 5 levels** *and* **on demand from the Pause menu** — so you're never
forced to wait for a checkpoint to power up.

Upgrades available:
- **Power Shot** — +1 shot damage
- **Rapid Blaster** — fire faster
- **Extra Barrel** — +1 bolt per shot
- **Nimble** — shrink your hitbox
- **Spirit Magnet** — draw in pickups
- **Vitality** — buy an extra heart *(capped per run)*

The store shows your live credit balance, each upgrade's level, and what you can afford at
a glance.

## Play-to-Earn ($utoñ)

utoñ is powered by the **$utoñ** token on **Solana**.

- **Play as guest** to jump straight in, or **connect a wallet** to compete for rewards.
- When you connect a wallet, your **score and wallet address** are submitted to the
  **leaderboard** at the end of a run.
- **Rewards are paid** by the team to top players' wallets — straight, simple,
  and transparent.



No deposit, no risk, no gas to play — you only ever need to **play well** and **share your
address** to earn.

## Tech

- **Single self-contained `index.html`** — all art, sound and logic in one file.
- **HTML5 Canvas** rendering with procedurally-drawn skylines and characters (no image assets).
- **Procedural WebAudio** music and sound effects (no audio files).
- **No build step, no dependencies, no framework** — just open it.
- **Mobile-first**, touch-optimised controls.
